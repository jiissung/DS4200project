This visualization displays student performance using a treemap that hierarchically groups the data by socioeconomic factors, including income level, access to resources, and motivation. Each rectangle represents a subgroup, with its size corresponding to the number of students and its color indicating the average exam score. The dataset is dynamically aggregated across these nested categories, allowing users to observe how combinations of factors jointly relate to performance. The treemap structure enables intuitive comparison across groups, highlighting patterns in both group size and average scores within different socioeconomic contexts.
