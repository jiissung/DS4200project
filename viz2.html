Impact of Factors on Exam Score by Motivation Level

This visualization shows that hours studied and tutoring sessions have the strongest positive relationship with exam scores, as increases in these factors are associated with noticeable gains in performance. The upward trends suggest that students who dedicate more time to studying or receive additional academic support tend to achieve higher scores. While other factors show weaker or more inconsistent relationships, these two stand out as the most impactful in improving outcomes
